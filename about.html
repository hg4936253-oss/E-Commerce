Our Story

ShopZone was born in 2020 in a small room in Bangalore. We started with just 50 products and a dream — to make quality products accessible to every Indian household.

Today, we serve over 2 million customers across 28 states, with 50,000+ products from trusted brands and local sellers.

From Gwalior to Guwahati, from Mumbai to Munger — ShopZone is your local shop, gone global.
